# Privacy Policy - SOROERU

Last updated: February 22, 2026

## Introduction

SOROERU ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data We Access

### Photos
- The App accesses photos you select in order to edit and unify their color tones.
- Photo data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers.

### Preset Settings
- Editing presets you save are stored in your device's local storage (UserDefaults).
- This data is not transmitted externally.

### Analytics and Diagnostics
The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect the following data for the purpose of improving app quality:

- **Crash logs**: Error information when the app crashes (device state, OS version, error details, etc.)
- **Diagnostics**: App performance data
- **App interactions**: Usage patterns such as screen transitions and feature usage (e.g., photo selection, export counts)

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally. It is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.)
- Location data
- Advertising identifiers
- Any other personal information

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Analytics** (Google LLC): Analysis of app usage
- **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google LLC): Collection and analysis of crash reports

For information about how these services handle data, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Photo data and preset settings are stored only on your device.
- Deleting the App will completely remove all data from your device.
- Analytics and diagnostics data collected by Firebase is stored on Google's servers and managed according to Google's data retention policies.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
